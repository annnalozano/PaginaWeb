Design Studio II

Design Autoethnography:

  • Describe and systematically analyze personal experiences to understand socio-technical systems.
  • Reflection and self-evaluation through autoethnography.
  • Autoethnographic practices = self-reflexive.
  • Responsibility, accountability, transparency, empathy, and positionality.
  • A personal vision to design.

Main Qualities:

  1. Defined study boundaries: Being able to describe the limits of the study.
  2. Authenticity: Being personally involved, thoroughness for validity.
  3. Plausibility or Scholarship: How is it relevant to ongoing research?
  4. Criticality: Imagining ways of thinking and acting differently.
  5. Self-revealing communication: Be honest with your findings. Communicate in an open and transparent way.
  6. Ability to Generalize: How does your 1PP research relate to others? How do I scale-up from the “self”?
